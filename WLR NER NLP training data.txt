The WLR is a system of subsea tensioners
It is a system comprising of 5 individual assets
During Wellhead Load relief operations, one unit remains on the ships deck
Oceaneering designed and built the WLRS
For maintenence, the workshop refers to a WLR task template
WLRS tasks templates are unique to the system
Four WLR tensioners are used to stablise other systems to a wellhead
The Wellhead Lod relief system is used in Brazil
There are 5 WLRS in Aberdeen
Norway designed and built the Wellhead Load Relief system
For outdoor storage, the WLRS should be wrapped in tarpaulin
